## 2. Defining Key Terms
To ensure clarity for all readers, we have defined the specific audio features used in this analysis below. These metrics are generated by Spotify's audio analysis algorithms:

* BPM (Beats Per Minute): The tempo of the track. Higher values imply a faster song.
* Energy: A measure of intensity and activity. Energetic tracks feel fast, loud, and noisy (e.g., Death Metal has high energy, Bach has low energy).
* Valence: A measure of musical "positiveness." High valence sounds happy or cheerful; low valence sounds sad or angry.
* Acousticness: A confidence measure of whether the track is acoustic (using non-electronic instruments).
* Loudness (dB): The overall volume of the track in decibels.
* Danceability: Describes how suitable a track is for dancing based on tempo, rhythm stability, and beat strength.
